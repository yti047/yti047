'PREP'

PREP offers recipes for people living alone.

You can find easy recipes that are easy to cook on your own, and you can share your own dishes and find the location of side dishes stores around you.

Make a Warm Meal with PREP!

INFORMATION

Why we made 'PREP'

As the number of single-person households living alone increased, I felt the need for a site that collects recipes for people living alone.

You can do these things with 'PREP'

First, you can get receipts

Second, you can share your meals

Lastly, you can find side dish store

The target we think ...

office workers, students, people who are not good at cooking, and people who have difficulty cooking

RECEIPT

Tomato pasta

Ingredients: pasta sauce, pasta, garlic, olive oil, a little salt, cheese

First: Slice garlic thinly and prepare.

Second: Add a little salt to the water and boil the pasta noodles. If you add a little oil, you can prevent noodles from sticking.

Third: Put olive oil in a pan and stir-fry garlic, then pour the sauce.

Fourth: Add noodles to the finished sauce and fry them briefly to finish.

Soy bean soup

Ingredients: Soy bean soup sauce, zucchini, onion, potato, green onion, tofu

First: Boil 500ml of water.

Second: Undo the sauce for the soybean paste stew. If not, put 1 spoon of soybean paste and 1 spoon of red pepper paste in anchovy stock and boil it.

Third: Add the prepared vegetables and boil them together, and you're done.

Sandwich

Ingredients: Two slices of bread, Lucola, honey, sliced ham, tomato, half an apple, brie cheese, mustard, ketchup

First: Wash and prepare ingredients that need to be washed, and slice apples and tomatoes thinly

Second: ketchup on one side of the bread and mustard on the other side.

Third: Step up the ingredients, put the apple on top at the end and sprinkle with honey.

Last: Cover the rest of the bread, tie it tightly with a wrap or string, and it's done.

Fried rice

Ingredients: carrot, potato, onion, zucchini, egg, oil, rice, salt, soy sauce, oyster sauce

First: Chop carrots, potatoes, onions and zucchini

Second: Grease the pan and stir-fry the chopped vegetables. Stir-fry and sprinkle salt

Third: When the onion is transparent, add a little soy sauce and a spoonful of oyster sauce

Fourth: Reduce the heat to low and mix the amount of rice you have to eat

Last: Stir-fry a little more on low heat, add fried eggs to your liking, and you're done.

Tteokbokki

Ingredients: Tteokbokki rice cake, fish cake, cabbage, water 600ml, red pepper powder, red pepper paste, sugar, soy sauce, starch syrup

First: make a sauce by mixing two spoons of red pepper powder, one spoon of red pepper paste, two spoons of sugar, one spoon of soy sauce, and one spoon of starch syrup.

Second: Remove the tteokbokki rice cake and soak it in water.

Third: Boil 600ml of water. If you have broth, you can use it.

Fourth: When the sauce comes to a boil, add the prepared rice cake, fish cake, and cabbage. Boil once and it's done.

Rolled Omelet

Ingredients: 2 eggs, carrot, onion, pumpkin, salt, pepper, oil

First: Chop carrots, onions, pumpkins, etc.

Second: Beat 2 eggs well and mix chopped vegetables.

Third: Grease the pan and pour the egg water thinly.

Fourth: When it's cooked, pour egg water into the rest and connect it.

Fifth: Roll it and it's over.

Tofu sushi

Ingredients: Fried tofu, rice, a little vinegar, sugar, furigake

First: After cooling the rice a little, add water mixed 1:1 with vinegar and sugar.

Second: Mix rice with furigake.

Third: Take the shape of a triangular fried tofu and put rice inside.

Fourth: Top with the toppings you want to put up according to your preference, and you're done.

French toast

Ingredients: bread, oil, eggs, sugar, sugar powder, maple syrup

First: Beat the eggs to make egg water. Cut the edges of the bread and soak it.

Second: Oil a pan and place a bread with egg water on it. Sprinkle sugar on it when it's baking and it creates a coating, making it even more delicious.

Third: Cook well evenly and transfer to a plate and top with sugar powder.

Fourth: eat with maple syrup on your preference.
